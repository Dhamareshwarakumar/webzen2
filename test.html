1

Discovery

The team will do their research and will take all the necessary steps so that we can make an accurate plan for your project.

2

Wire framing

Before we reach the design prototype stage wireframes will be created to plot out the website user flow and structure.

3

Design

In an end to end collaborative process a bespoke design for your website will be created in Photoshop

4

Development

A digital version will be developed on our staging server, using the final designs

5

Population

Function will be established and the site populated with content to meet the project requirements

6

Testing

This is the stage that consists of full testing with Q&A as well as testing for interoperability to provide a performance that is always optimal
